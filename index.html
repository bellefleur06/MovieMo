Will be forever my favorite out of all the Studio Ghibli movies I watched. The visuals, characters, soundtrack and the plot is majestic. The movie gave me the feels of being a kid in a big adventure, along with friends and family supporting you. I love how they made you love the protagonists as they slowly grew mature as the story progressed. The soundtrack in the movie sounds so beautiful especially the opening theme and Pazu's trumpet solo. I can't help imagining myself standing in the castle in the sky just admiring the view. This was the first Studio Ghibli movie I watched and it was worth it.
